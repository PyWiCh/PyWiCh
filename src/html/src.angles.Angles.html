def __init__(self, phi, theta):

Angles class cosntructor.

This method constructs an Angles object given the azimuth and inclination angles. The azimuth (phi) is measure in the horizontal plane xy from the axe x positive to axe y positive and the inclination (theta) is measure in the vertical plane from axe z positive to the horizontal plane.
