def _set_params(self, d2d, h_MS, los):

This method sets the scenario parameters.

It sets for LOS and NLOS condition the correlation distance of the LSP parameters, the mean and variance of the LSP parameters and the crosscorrelation matrix between LSP parameters. The LSP parameters are: The Delay Spread (DS), Azimuth angle of arrival spread (AOA_AZS or ASA azimuth spread arrival), Azimuth angle of departure spread (AOD_AZS or ASD), Shadowing (Shadow), elevation angle of departure spread(AOD_ELS or ZSD zenith spread departure), elevation angle of arrival spread (AOA_ELS or ZSD), Ricean K factor(K). See Table 7.5-6 Part-1.

Parameters
d2d:float.The distance between the BS and MS positions. Default 10.
h_​MS:float.The MS antenna height. Default 2.
